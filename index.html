Beatflare Music Player Project

A modern, open-source music player with a sleek interface, smart songs management, a powerful equalizer, dark mode, and a surprise Party Mode, all for the perfect music vibe!

  • ▶️ Play, Pause & Skip with ease ▶️
  • 📂 Supports multiple audio formats 📂
  • 🌙 Light & Dark Mode 🌙
  • 🎨 Multiple Color Themes
  • 🎚️ Customizable equalizer & Audio Effects 🎚️
  • 🔊 Audio Visualizer
  • 📃 Effortless Songs management 📃
  • 🎉 Party Mode 🎉
